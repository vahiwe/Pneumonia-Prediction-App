About App

Transforming Healthcare in Africa through Artificial Intelligence

Emerging technologies hold great possibilities to improving the current state of healthcare in Africa.

There is a huge deficit in the ratio of Doctors to patients in Africa and this seriously reduces the accesss Africans have to quality healthcare

PneumoDoc makes use of computer vision technologies to examine X-rays scans and make inferences from scans if a patient has Pnuemonia or not.

PnuemoDoc is an X-ray AI assistant for Doctors and Radiologist to help detect Pnuemonia in scans with great accuracy.
